Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) Degree

About

A Three (03) Year Degree Program including Six (06) Semester. Selection procedure for BCA program is on Merit Basis.

A BCA degree is a solid foundation for a career in the IT industry. It equips students with essential technical skills, problem-solving abilities, and practical experience needed to excel in various roles. Whether entering the workforce directly or pursuing further studies, BCA graduates have a wide range of opportunities to explore and succeed in the dynamic field of computer science.

Eligibility Criteria

Passed Higher Secondary (10+2) examination securing minimum of 45% marks in aggregate and passing Mathematics/ Commercial Arithmetic /Statistics as one of the subjects. Relaxation of 5% marks will be given to the candidates of reserved categories.

Intake Capacity

60 (6 Endowments)

Curriculum

The BCA curriculum includes a comprehensive mix of theoretical and practical subjects related to computer science. Our program covers:

  • Programming Languages (C, C++, Java, Python)
  • Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Database Management Systems
  • Web Development
  • Operating Systems
  • Software Engineering
